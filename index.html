About San Marino

San Marino, also known as the Most Serene Repuplic of San Marino, is an enclaved microstate surrounded by Italy,situated on the Italian Peninsula on the north-eastern side of the Apennine
Mountains.

Location of San Marino

Flag of San Marino

The flag of San Marino incorporates the Coat of Arms. The towers symbolise the three citadels of San Marino (La Guaita, La Cesta and La Montale), while the hills represent the three summits of Monte Titano. The motto "LIBERTAS" is Latin for "Freedom".

Flag of San Marino

The Fortress of Guaita on Mount Titano

The Guaita fortress is the oldest of the three towers constructed on Monte Titano, and the most famous. It was built in the 11th century and served briefly as a prison.
